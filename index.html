About Me

Hi! My name is Alyssa and I created this little site for my class on webdesign! I am 24 and a second year student at the University of Michgian focusing on Libraries and Archives. looking towards my career I would like to be a public librarian, maybe even a children's librarian. I am unsure at the moment exactly what I want to be which I have discovered is quite normal for this degree.

I decided to dedicate this website to myself and things that interest me. The other pages on here are from my most recent travel experience, a trip I took to Scotland in 2019. Next is a page devoted to my recent crochet projects. I have a passion for crochet and anychance I can show off my creations I will take it. Finally we have the current reads page. As an aspiring librarian I feel this is a necessary catagory.
